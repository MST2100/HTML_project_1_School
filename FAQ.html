FAQ for COVID-19

These questions have frequently been asked by students and employees in the period 2020-2021.
If you do not find any answer to your questions, please write us as Helpdesk@cbs.dk

I am a student what should I know?

Question Answer
Can I enter the University? Yes, if you are vaccinated or have a negative COVID-19 test within 72-hours
What if I am tested positive for COVID-19? Follow the governments rules and updates regarding COVID-19
Do I need to wear the mask? Yes, you need to wear the mask when not sitting down at a table

I am an employee what should I know?

Question Answer
Can I work at my office? Yes, if you have a negative COVID-19 test or are vaccinated
Do I need to wear the mask? Yes, you need to wear the mask when walking in the hallways,
but not when giving lectures unless you have students standing within 1-meter to you
